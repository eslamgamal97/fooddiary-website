About Food Diary

Food Diary was created to help people maintain healthier eating habits through simple, consistent meal tracking. Whether you're managing a health condition, trying to lose weight, or simply want to be more mindful of your nutrition, our app provides the tools you need to succeed.

With seamless Google integration, your meal data is always accessible and can be easily shared with nutritionists, doctors, or family members. We believe that tracking your food should be as simple as eating it.
