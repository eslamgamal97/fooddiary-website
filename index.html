About Food Diary

Food Diary was created to help people maintain healthier eating habits through simple, consistent meal tracking. Whether you're managing a health condition, trying to lose weight, or simply want to be more mindful of your nutrition, our app provides the tools you need to succeed.

With seamless Google integration, your meal data is always accessible and can be easily shared with nutritionists, doctors, or family members. We believe that tracking your food should be as simple as eating it.

Privacy Policy for Food Diary App

Last updated: August 28, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Food Diary App collects the following information:

  • Google Account Information: Email address and profile name when you sign in with Google
  • Meal Data: Food items, categories (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks), and timestamps that you manually enter
  • Usage Data: App performance and crash reports to improve the service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and maintain your personal food diary
  • Sync your meal data across your devices
  • Create a personalized Google Spreadsheet in your Google Drive
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve the app's functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely:

  • Google Sheets: Your meal data is stored in a spreadsheet in your own Google Drive
  • Local Device: Temporary data is stored locally on your device
  • Firebase: Authentication data is handled securely by Google Firebase

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With Google services (Sheets, Drive, Authentication) as necessary for app functionality
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights

5. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For user authentication
  • Google Sheets API: To create and manage your food diary spreadsheet
  • Google Drive API: To save spreadsheets in your Google Drive
  • Firebase: For authentication and app analytics

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data within the app and your Google Spreadsheet
  • Delete: Remove meals from your diary or delete your entire spreadsheet
  • Export: Download your data from your Google Spreadsheet
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect the app from your Google account at any time

7. Data Retention

Your meal data is retained:

  • In your Google Spreadsheet until you delete it
  • Locally on your device until you uninstall the app
  • Authentication data is managed by Google Firebase

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@fooddiary.app
  • Through the app: Use the feedback option in the navigation menu

11. Permissions Explanation

Internet Access: Required to sync data with Google Sheets and authenticate with Google

Network State: To check connectivity before attempting sync operations

Google Account Access: To create and access your personal food diary spreadsheet

This privacy policy is effective as of August 28, 2025, and was last updated on August 28, 2025.
